PCHS
Programming Team

The Panther Creek High School Programming Team represents the school in various computer science competitions. We welcome programmers of all levels to join and compete in contests that suit their skill levels. Some of the competitions we will participate in include:

  • USACO (USA Computing Olympiad)
  • CofC HSPC (College of Charleston High School Programming Competition)
  • UNC Asheville HSPC
  • Virginia Tech HSPC
  • ACM@UVa HSPC
  • Google Code Jam
  • Google Code-in

Besides competing, we will also help sharpen your skills with optional online practice rounds. We will also try to host a regional competition for high schools in the RTP region to compete.
